SentiShop

Analyze Lazada product reviews to make smarter buying decisions.

View on GitHub Back to Portfolio

Overview

SentiShop collects and analyzes customer reviews from Lazada, applying natural language processing to determine overall sentiment and highlight insights that help shoppers purchase with confidence.

The app provides a clean dashboard summarizing positive and negative trends for each product, with drill-down to individual review detail.
