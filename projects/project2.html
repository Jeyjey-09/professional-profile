Overview

BiteBox helps indecisive diners by surfacing curated restaurant picks aligned with taste and budget. Set your preferences and location and let BiteBox do the picking for you.

Whether you're exploring a new city or craving something different, BiteBox reduces choice fatigue and gets you to a great meal faster.

Project Info

  • Role: Backend Developer
  • Type: Mobile Application
  • Stack: React, Google Places API, HTML/CSS/JS
